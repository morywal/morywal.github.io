AI Retro Game Helper

My Role: Solo Developer & Designer
Date Created: May 2025
Context: Senior Capstone Project (CLU)
Skills: Python, AI (OpenAI API), UI (Tkinter), API Integration, JSON

The Project: A Spoiler-Free AI Helper

The Problem: I often start a game, run out of time to play, and put it down for months. When I come back, I remember the core story but have forgotten crucial details like names, places, and side quests.

My Solution: I built a standalone AI chatbot that integrates with the RetroArch emulator. The bot reads data from the emulator's console and tracks the player's progress. When the player asks a question, the AI cross-references their progress with a game-data (JSON) file to provide a spoiler-free, context-aware answer.

The Retro Helper Integration

The application was built as an add-on to RetroArch, which required me to research its documentation to understand how to read its console output in real-time.
