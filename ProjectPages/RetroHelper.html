The Fusion Fest

The Project: A Multimedia Jam

My Role: Co-Founder, Event Designer, & Project Manager

I co-founded and designed 'Fusion Fest,' a 48-hour multimedia jam, to foster a new culture of collaboration within the CLU Multimedia program. My role involved pitching the concept to faculty, designing the event structure, and mentoring student teams. The event was a major success and is now an annual tradition.

The Process: From Pitch to Event

The Challenge: The multimedia program had many talented students but lacked a high-energy, collaborative event to bring them together and build community.

My Solution: I designed 'Fusion Fest' to solve this. My responsibilities included:

  • Writing the initial pitch and design document to get faculty buy-in.
  • Designing the theme, creative constraints, and judging criteria.
  • Structuring the 48-hour schedule to balance work-time with mentorship.
  • Managing event logistics and communication with student teams.
