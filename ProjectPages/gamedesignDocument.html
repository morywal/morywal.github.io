The Project: a Game Design Document

This was a short day assingment to quickly design, define, and document a fun, replayable 'core loop' for any sort of party game. We chose a Jackbox inspired party game.

The Process and Goals

My design goal was to create a simple, easy-to-learn game. The main challenge was speed. I had to prioritize the most critical parts of a GDD to get the point across.

The Impact

This project was a great exercise in scope and speed. It taught me how to prioritize the absolute essentials of a design and communicate a game idea clearly and concisely under a tight deadline. I chose to include this despite the depth to show the importance of understanding a short goal and how to feasibly execute a blue sky pitch into something that is realistic given time.
