Loud House Animatic

My Role: Animatic Editor
Date Created: October 2023
Context: Personal Initiative Project (CLU Guest Speaker)
Tech Stack: Adobe Premiere Pro

The Project

A guest speaker visited my class, I took the initiative to reach out and ask for sample materials to practice with. He provided a professional script and storyboard files for the intro of an episode of *The Loud House*. I then taught myself Adobe Premiere Pro to edit these assets, including sound effects and timing, into a complete 1-minute animatic.

The Process & Goals

My goal was to gain hands-on experience in a professional animation pipeline. I wanted to learn the role of an animatic editor in setting the pace, timing, and "feel" of an episode before final animation.

The main challenge was learning Premiere Pro from scratch. I researched professional animatic workflows to understand how to time out storyboard panels and integrate audio to simulate the final product's energy.

Impact & Reflection

This project was a fantastic exercise in personal initiative. It taught me how to independently learn an industry-standard tool (Premiere Pro) and successfully apply it to a real-world professional workflow. It solidified my understanding of how pacing and editing form the blueprint of all animated storytelling.
